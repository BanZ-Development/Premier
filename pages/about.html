About Us

Brief History

Premier Transportation was started by Greg and B.J. Chandler on January 1st, 1989. Their goal was to provide the best possible service for their customers while creating a great place for employees and owner operators to work. They began with 5000 square feet of office and warehouse, with three owner operators and one local driver.

Today, Premier Transportation is one of the most respected companies in the specialized carrier industry, with nearly 100 employees and owner operators ready to serve you throughout the United States and Canada. Premier specializes in meeting the needs of the machine tool and other specialized industries. You can contact them at any of their three locations: California, Illinois, and North Carolina. Call Premier Transportation.

Attracting Talent

Premier Transportation is always interested in quality drivers. We provide a number of pay options along with great benefits and first class equipment. Drivers should be at least 25 years of age and have at least two years of driving experience and a clean record. Premier hires local, regional and long distance drivers. PremierTWI Careers

Partnerships with Owner Operators

Premier Transportation is always interested in quality owner operators. Premier has a contract which is thorough, easy to understand, and provides the qualified owner operator an opportunity to make great money. PremierTWI Careers
