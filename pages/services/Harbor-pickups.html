Harbor Pick-Ups

Harbor Pick-Ups in CA

At our main office in Long Beach, CA we offer a full range of services for the ports of Los Angeles and Long Beach. We can pick up both loose and containerized cargo. We can unload, de-Van, and store your cargo or ship it to a final destination. All you have to do is instruct your customs broker to provide us a delivery order and a packing list. We will then check with the port continually for the cargo to become available, make the necessary appointments, and pick up your cargo before the free time expires. Our warehouse is located within the harbor’s “Overweight Corridor” which allows us to pick up some overweight containers.

Harbor Pick-up Throughout the U.S.

Premier can also pick up your shipments from other U.S. ports. We frequently pick up at the ports of NJ/ NY, Baltimore MD, Norfolk VA, Charleston SC, and Savannah GA
